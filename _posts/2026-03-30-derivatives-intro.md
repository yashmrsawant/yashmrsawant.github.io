### The Power Rule

To find the derivative of a function like $f(x) = x^n$, we use the power rule.

The formula is:
$$f'(x) = n \cdot x^{n-1}$$

**Example:**
If $f(x) = x^3$, then $f'(x) = 3x^2$.
